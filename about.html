About SamiMart

Your trusted neighbourhood supermarket since 2025.

Our Story

SamiMart was founded in 2025 with a simple mission — bring fresh, quality groceries to every household at affordable prices. What started as a small corner shop has grown into a beloved community supermarket serving thousands of families every week.

We source directly from local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure that every product on our shelves meets the highest standards of freshness and quality.

Our Mission & Values

Customer First

Every decision we make is guided by what is best for our customers and community.

Fresh & Natural

We are committed to offering the freshest produce, free from unnecessary additives.

Community

We support local farmers, hire locally, and give back to the community we serve.
